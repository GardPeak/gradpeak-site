Seaholme

Seaholme is an Architect designed 3 storey holiday house enjoying a prominent location at the west end of Earlsferry with fabulous views over the golf course inland and over Elie Bay.

Main Contractor, Demolition, Build

A Complete New Construction

The Brief

As Main Contractor Gradual Peak was responsible for all stages of the build from demolition of existing property to the handover of the completed home. All within the confines of a site barely 8m wide.

All aspects of the design from the basement to the boat shaped upper floor presented demanding construction methods and the required quality of finish was particularly high.
