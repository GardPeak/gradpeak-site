Blebocraigs

This project combined the bespoke extension and attic conversion of a previously redeveloped cottage.

Extension, Conversion

Extend and Convert

The Brief

Most of the work was undertaken with the client in residence. The extension involved the construction of an exposed oak roof (manufactured in our workshops).

The contract combined several special features including a luxury ensuite wet room, spiral staircase with curved glass block wall and fully automated roof lights.
