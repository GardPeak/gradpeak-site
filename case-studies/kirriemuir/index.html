Gradual Peak

Kirriemuir

Design & Build of a luxury 4 bedroom mill conversion featuring original water wheel and stunning views of Angus Glen, near Kirriemuir.

Development, Build

A Design and Build

The Brief

This was a development project for Gradual Peak undertaken in the midst of the recession. To bring life back to the property in the form of a holiday/family home.

The best features of this ruined mill were retained/extended and combined with elements of contemporary design.
