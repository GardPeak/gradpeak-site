Gradual Peak

Gradual Peak is a Building and Development company on a mission to bring professionalism, innovation, quality and integrity to the construction market.

What we do

The real question is, what don't we do? We provide Cost Plans to clients and architects. We can also provide a one stop solution for design and build with a professional and cost effective Architectural/Engineering service. Projects range from small extensions to multiple house developments including renovations and demolitions.

Our in house Architectural/Engineering team can undertake surveys, CAD drawings and applications for Planning Consents, Building Warrants and Utility Consents ensuring the journey from concept to reality is as smooth as possible.

Who we are

We are a privately owned Building and Development company.

Our founding ethos is to bring professionalism, innovation, quality and integrity to the construction market. To facilitate the creation of unique buildings for individual clients with the minimum possible disruption to their daily lives.

Driven from the top down our staff share a passion for problem solving and the satisfaction that comes from completing difficult projects to a high standard.
